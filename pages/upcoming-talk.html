A overview of star formation

The fundamental motivation for the existence of the field of astronomy is the presence of stars in the night sky. As such, it is imperative to understand the formation and evolution of the stellar sources which has fascinated us since times immemorial. The same is also essential for understanding the dynamics of our Galaxy. In this talk, I will give an overview of the field of star formation, the methodologies deployed to study the field, and some of the active areas of investigation, with an emphasis on high-mass star formation.
